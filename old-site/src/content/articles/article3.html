What is Rameelo?

Rameelo is a non-profit organization started by Heran Patel, aka HP Maharaja, and co-founded by Bhrugesh Desai in 2021. Now in our 5th year, we aim to elevate Rameelo to new heights, delivering cultural experiences through amazing events. Our mission is to preserve and grow the ancient traditions of India for future generations. At this retreat, we hosted some of our executive team members including: + Heran Patel - Founder & CEO + Bhrugesh Desai - Found & COO + Parth Rana + Suchit Desai + Rohan Chadha + Mukund Gharmalkar
