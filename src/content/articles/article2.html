Money is constantly flowing

Money is constantly flowing around the world, and to put things into perspective, if every person on the planet spends $5 in a day, then the volume of transactions is $41 billion changing hands per day! Of course, this is probably just the cost of a coffee, and people end up spending a lot more than that, but that should give you a solid idea of how much money is flowing around the world. This flow is also an avenue for individuals, businesses, and corporations to accrue more value! Opportunity is immense, but at what cost?

With this much money flowing, the average person's limiting mindset degrades their thinking that the goal should be $100K per year—which, put into the perspective of daily transactions, is only 0.00024% of the daily $5 coffee transaction around the globe. Why limit the mindset to money when there is so much to look forward to? This example is not made to convince you that you can make more money, although that could be the case, but rather that money, in essence, is always flowing and changes hands faster than you can fathom.

Purpose is deeper

So, then you come to the point at which you ask yourself, how does money flow more through you, and how can you paint a bigger story about yourself? The answer is purpose. Without a purpose, your life is incomplete, and not only do you miss out on the possibilities of having more financial flow, but you also lose yourself in the bigger picture of Earth.

The more people learn, understand, and align with your purpose, the more financial flow you will have in life!

Purpose is more valuable than money

When you have a purpose, it will attract the right individuals to join your purpose, seeing your passion, hard work, and commitment to consistently work on it. The attraction of more individuals will bring financial flow through you, after which you can channel those funds to progress your purpose in life.

Without pure intent, you won't be changing a thing!

It's a cycle

The circle of acquiring financial flow (money) and enhancing your purpose is never-ending for as long as you keep the wheel moving! You have to build enough momentum with the things you create while constantly fueling the fire of your purpose. Because we are finite beings, there will be an end to this life, and the impact you create will leave a lasting legacy.

The Future

The future is bright, and you have the power now to change the wheel of purpose and financial flow. Take small but consistent steps, and don't be afraid to take the risks that 100% align with your purpose! The future holds many possibilities, but don't let the money mindset cloud your purpose because money will follow your purpose.
