# Rameelo Retreat Recapped · Jan 2025

![Retreat Group Photo](https://rameelo-2024.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/rameelo-retreat/group-2.jpg)

## What Is Rameelo?

**Rameelo** is a non-profit organization founded in 2021 by **Heran Patel (HP Maharaja)** and **Bhrugesh Desai**.

Now in our **5th consecutive year**, our mission is simple yet powerful:

> **To preserve, grow, and modernize India’s ancient cultural traditions through immersive Raas Garba experiences.**

At the January 2025 retreat, we brought together a portion of our core leadership for three days of:

- strategic discussion  
- vision alignment  
- personal rejuvenation  
- team bonding  
- creative exploration

- **Bhrugesh Desai** — Co-Founder & COO

## Retreat Objectives

The purpose of the retreat was clear:  
**reflect, realign, and rebuild stronger for the next era of Rameelo.**

We analyzed:

- what worked  
- what didn’t  
- how to elevate our events  
- what systems we must fix  
- how to build a stronger culture  
- how to lead with purpose  

Despite the challenges of 2024, we walked away energized, aligned, and more capable than ever.

### **2024 Challenges**
- Poor financial outcomes  
- Venue spacing & parking issues  
- Lack of accurate attendee counts  
- Underperformance in experience delivery  
- Overspending in unnecessary areas  
- Missed execution of Vivah matchmaking  

### **2024 Successes**
- Top-tier celebrity artist experiences  
- Premium decor & unforgettable photo ops  
- Strong food vendor options  
- Expansion into new markets (Seattle)
